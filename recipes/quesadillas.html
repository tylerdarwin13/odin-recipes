Gluten-Free Chicken Quesadillas

Description

Chicken Quesadillas are an easy and satisfying meal, requiring little adjustment for us gluten-free eaters. This simple recipe is one of my favorites, and might soon be yours too.

Ingredients

  • Corn Tortillas - The easiest and best option for gluten-free tortillas.
  • Cooked Chicken - Rotisserie chicken is my favorite for this recipe, making it even easier than it already is!
  • Mexican Cheese Blend - Sharp cheddar is another great choice. Basically any shredded cheese will work, and it's worth it to experiment!
  • Preferred Cooking Oil - Any standard cooking oil works, but I reccommend butter!
  • Seasoning - Salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Other seasonings are optional.

Steps

  1. Heat pan to medium heat and add cooking oil. Add the bottom tortilla to pan. Note - you can make multiple quesadillas in a single pan if its big enough. Just make sure they don't overlap!
  2. Add a thin layer of cheese on top of the tortillas.
  3. Top the cheese with chicken. Season the half-made quesadilla with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  4. Top with a heavy serving of cheese, followed by the other tortilla.
  5. When the bottom layer of cheese is melted, flip the quesadilla. Turn as needed until the tortillas and cheese are warm and crispy on both sides.
  6. Cut into quarters, plate, and enjoy!
